How it works
Time for Fresh Thinking about what, why & how you eat?

The Think Fresh programme offers you 10 weekly local group sessions plus 1 to 1 support and a network of new people to meet, share and laugh with. No weigh-ins, no fad diets just support, understanding and encouragement to help you reach the goals that matter to you.

Your relationship to food is unique to you, Think Fresh gets that. How we think and feel about food and about ourselves is key to what and how we eat. Think Fresh will give you the insight you may have been missing so why not find your local group and start today - Think Happy, Think Healthy, Think Fresh.
